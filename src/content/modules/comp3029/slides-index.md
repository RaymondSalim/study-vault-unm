### Cross-cutting Themes

- **Feature representation** -- from hand-crafted (SIFT, Haar) to learned (CNN features)
- **Matching and correspondence** -- relevant in SIFT matching, stereo, and optical flow
- **Scale and invariance** -- runs through SIFT, CNN pooling, and pyramid methods
