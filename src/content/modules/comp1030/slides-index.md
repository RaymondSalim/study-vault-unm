## Tips for Using Slides

- Lectures 1-4 provide the theoretical foundation -- make sure to master these before moving on
- Lectures 9-13 are heavily interlinked -- study them together as the "HACK computer" block
- Past exams often draw equally from number systems, Boolean logic, and assembly
