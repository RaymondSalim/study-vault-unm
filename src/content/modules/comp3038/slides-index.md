### Cross-cutting Themes

- **Bias-variance tradeoff** -- appears in every supervised model (regularisation, ensemble methods, early stopping)
- **Optimisation** -- gradient descent connects linear models, SVMs, and neural networks
- **Model selection** -- evaluation methodology (topic 8) applies to all algorithms
