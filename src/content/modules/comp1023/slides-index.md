## Lecture Slides Overview

| Lecture | Topic | Key Concepts | Exam Weight |
|---------|-------|--------------|-------------|
| 1-2 | SDLC and Processes | Waterfall model, V-model, iterative development, Agile methodology, Scrum framework, Kanban, sprints, roles | 20% |

## Notes

- UML diagrams (class + sequence) make up ~30% of the exam — practise drawing them by hand.
- SDLC and Processes questions often ask you to compare/contrast methodologies — prepare arguments for both sides.
- Requirements and Use Cases are often tested together in scenario-based questions.
- OOP Design Principles may appear as "identify the problem" questions with code snippets.
- Testing is often combined with other topics (e.g., "how would you test this use case?").
