# Slides Index

| Lecture | Topic | Key Concepts | Exam Weight |
|---------|-------|--------------|-------------|

## Tips for Using Slides

- SQL lectures (5-9) carry the most marks -- practise writing queries by hand, not just reading examples
- Normalisation (10-11) requires understanding FDs deeply; work through decomposition exercises step by step
- ER modelling appears early and is often the first exam question -- get the notation right
- Transactions are conceptual; focus on understanding ACID and identifying concurrency anomalies
