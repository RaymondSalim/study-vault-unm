## Revision Priority

- **Must know (High):** Lectures 1, 4, 5 -- propositional logic foundations and predicate logic with quantifiers
- **Should know (Medium):** Lectures 2, 3, 7, 8, 9 -- algebraic manipulation, structural induction on various types
- **Lower priority (Low):** Lecture 6 -- Boolean data type (usually tested lightly)
- **Focus areas:** Natural deduction proofs (covered across lectures), semantic tableaux, quantifier manipulation, structural induction on lists and trees

**Note:** Natural deduction rules are applied throughout multiple lectures. Ensure you can construct proofs using all introduction/elimination rules regardless of which lecture introduced them.
