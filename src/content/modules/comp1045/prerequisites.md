# Prerequisites

## What You Need Before This Module

- **A-Level Maths (or equivalent)** -- confident with algebra, basic differentiation and integration, simultaneous equations
- **COMP1045 Maths for CS 1 (or equivalent)** -- vectors, basic matrices, introductory probability
- **Comfort with mathematical notation** -- summation ($\Sigma$), factorial, function composition
- **Basic trigonometry** -- sin, cos, tan values and identities

## Prior Modules That Help

| Module | How it helps |

| A-Level Mathematics | Differentiation, integration, series, basic probability |
| A-Level Further Mathematics (if taken) | Matrices, complex numbers, ODEs -- gives a strong head start |

## Quick Self-Test

Answer these 5 questions to check your readiness:

1. **Differentiate $f(x) = 3x^2 + 2x - 1$.** (Expected: $6x + 2$)
2. **Integrate $\int 2x \, dx$.** (Expected: $x^2 + C$)
3. **What is $\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{pmatrix} \begin{pmatrix} 5 \\ 6 \end{pmatrix}$?** (Expected: $\begin{pmatrix} 17 \\ 39 \end{pmatrix}$)
4. **What is 5! (5 factorial)?** (Expected: 120)
5. **If P(A) = 0.4 and P(B) = 0.3, and A, B are independent, what is P(A and B)?** (Expected: 0.12)

If you can answer at least 4 correctly, you are well-prepared. If not, revise A-Level differentiation, integration, and matrix multiplication before the module starts.
