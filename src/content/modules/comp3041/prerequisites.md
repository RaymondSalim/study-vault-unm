### Self-Test: Are You Ready?

This module requires willingness to discuss and debate rather than specific technical prerequisites. Consider whether you can:

1. **Identify an ethical dilemma in a technology scenario** — e.g., "A company collects user location data to improve their map service. What ethical concerns arise?"
2. **Distinguish between legal and ethical** — "Something can be legal but unethical, or illegal but ethical. Give an example of each."
3. **Name a real-world case of technology causing harm** — e.g., Facebook/Cambridge Analytica, Therac-25, algorithmic bias
4. **Consider multiple perspectives** — Given a scenario, can you argue both sides before reaching a conclusion?
5. **Write a structured argument** — Can you present a claim, provide evidence, and reach a conclusion?
