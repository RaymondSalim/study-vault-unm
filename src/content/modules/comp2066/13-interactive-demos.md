## Interactive Tools

Experiment with fundamental algorithms in your browser.

**Available demos:**
- **Sorting Visualiser** — choose Bubble, Selection, Insertion, Merge, or Quick sort. Step through one operation at a time or animate at adjustable speed.
- **Graph Traversal** — watch BFS and DFS explore a graph node by node. See the queue/stack state at each step.
